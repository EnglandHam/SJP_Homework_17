GRA Project Overview (Google Presentation)

Greyhound Rescue Austin Nonprofit Project

Complete redesign of GRA website for high fiedelity website and mobile. The current GRA website hinders the mission of the organization. Users struggle to use the website for main activities (adopting, fostering, volunteering). The website projects a feeling of untrustworthiness about the organization due to outdated visuals and lack of professionalism. Our team redesigned the GRA website by centering websites’ goals around users’ primary needs (adopting, fostering, volunteering) and displaying the legitimacy of the site.
